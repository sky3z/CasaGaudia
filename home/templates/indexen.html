ARE YOU LOOKING FOR A FLAT IN THE CENTRE OF CASTELLO TESINO?

Good news then, because you have just found the right thing for you: The Casa Gaudia, offers a garden accommodation a few metres from the historic centre of Castello Tesino , a small village in the Dolomites at an altitude of 850m.

The flat with a balcony and mountain views features 1 bedroom, a living room, a flat-screen TV, a kitchen equipped with an oven and fridge, and 1 bathroom with bidet.

The ideal solution for a holiday in Trentino. of sport, relaxation or tranquillity!

A mini-market is available near the flat.

Casa Gaudia is the perfect place to take beautiful walks and explore the Tesino in the saddle of an E-Bike.
